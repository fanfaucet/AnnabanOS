# AnnabanOS Brand Identity

## Overview

AnnabanOS is the next-generation AI-native operating system, built at the intersection of human intent and machine intelligence. This document defines the visual and verbal identity for all AnnabanOS communications, products, and collaborations.

---

## Brand Name

**Primary:** AnnabanOS  
**Formal:** Annaban Operating System  
**Short:** Annaban

---

## Tagline

> *"Where Intelligence Meets Intent"*

---

## Brand Voice

- **Precise but approachable** — Technical depth without gatekeeping
- **Deterministic about process, humble about outcomes** — We control the pipeline, not the truth
- **Collaborative** — Built with partners, not in isolation
- **Future-facing** — Anticipating where AI governance needs to go

---

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Annaban Purple | `#6C5CE7` | Primary brand color, headers, key elements |
| Deep Void | `#0F0F1A` | Dark backgrounds, terminal themes |
| Signal Cyan | `#00D2D3` | Accent, links, interactive elements |
| Audit Amber | `#FDCB6E` | Warnings, highlights, secondary accents |
| Clean White | `#F5F6FA` | Light backgrounds, text on dark |
| Steel Grey | `#636E72` | Body text, secondary information |

---

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Inter or SF Pro Display — clean, modern, authoritative
- **Body:** Inter or system-ui — highly readable at all sizes
- **Code/Technical:** JetBrains Mono or SF Mono — monospaced for all code references

---

## Logo Usage

- Always maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 1x logo height)
- Use Annaban Purple on light backgrounds, Clean White on dark backgrounds
- Never distort, rotate, or apply effects to the logo
- The "OS" suffix may be dropped in informal contexts where the operating system context is implicit

---

## Verbal Guidelines

### Do
- "AnnabanOS routes your prompts through deterministic governance"
- "The orchestrator observes signals without claiming truth"
- "Built in collaboration with Kimi"

### Don't
- "AnnabanOS is an AI that knows the answer"
- "Our model guarantees correctness"
- "100% accurate governance"

---

## Collaboration Marks

When featuring the Kimi collaboration:
- Use: "AnnabanOS × Kimi" or "Built with Kimi"
- Include both brand marks with equal prominence
- Maintain the collaboration lockup on co-branded materials
